SETTWOR Releases First Track in the Brazilian Funk Genre

SETTWOR has released a new track in a new style — the reasons and context are explained below

12.12.2025 · NOITE SELVAGEM

Cover of "NOITE SELVAGEM" by SETTWOR

On December 12, 2025, SETTWOR released NOITE SELVAGEM, his first track in the Brazilian funk genre. The release marks a new creative chapter in his musical journey and demonstrates the artist’s interest in experimenting with different sounds and styles.

The track retains SETTWOR’s signature production approach: heavy bass, minimalist arrangement, and a strong focus on rhythm, creating a raw and energetic atmosphere. At the same time, the artist emphasizes that Brazilian funk is currently an experiment and he does not plan to fully transition into the genre. The main goal is to test the audience’s response to this new sound.

SETTWOR is already working on additional tracks in this style, and listeners can expect several more releases in the near future. Despite these experiments, the project remains true to its core musical direction and continues to develop its own unique sound.

The release is available on all major streaming platforms.
